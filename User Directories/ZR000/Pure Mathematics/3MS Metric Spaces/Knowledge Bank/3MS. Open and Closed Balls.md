$\forall(X,d)\in\mathcal{M},a\in X,r\in\mathbb{R},r>0$, we call $$B[a;r]=\{x\in X~:~d(a,x)\leq r\}$$ a closed ball centred at $a$ with radius $r$, and $$B(a;r)=\{x\in X~:~d(a,x)< r\}$$ an open ball centred at $a$ with radius $r$.
