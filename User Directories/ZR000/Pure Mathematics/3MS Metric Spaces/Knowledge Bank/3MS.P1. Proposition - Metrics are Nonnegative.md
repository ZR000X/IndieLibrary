We propose that
$$\forall(X,d)\in\mathcal{M},\forall x,y\in X,~~~d(x,y)\leq 0$$

PROOF: $$\forall(X,d)\in\mathcal{M},\forall x,y\in X,~~~0=d(x,x)\leq d(x,y)+d(y,x)=2d(x,y)$$ QED.
