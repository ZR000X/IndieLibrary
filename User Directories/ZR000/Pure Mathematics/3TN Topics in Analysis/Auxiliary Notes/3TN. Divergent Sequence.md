We say a sequence $(x_n)$ diverges if it has limit $\pm\infty$. To put these ideas precisely,

$$\lim_{n\to\infty}x_n=\infty~~\Leftrightarrow~~\forall K>0,\exists n_K\in\mathbb{N}:~n\geq n_K~\Rightarrow~x_n>K$$
$$\lim_{n\to\infty}x_n=-\infty~~\Leftrightarrow~~\forall K>0,\exists n_K\in\mathbb{N}:~n\geq n_K~\Rightarrow~x_n<-K$$
